Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

1) Site Name:

Jeonju Chamber of Commerce: This name represents the local business organization in the city of Jeonju, South Korea.

2) Site Purpose:

The Jeonju Chamber of Commerce aims to enhance the local business environment and promote economic growth. This will be achieved through organizing networking events, opportunity awareness campaigns, and community engagement efforts. As such, the website serves as a platform for members to access resources, events information, and support services.

3) Scenarios:

Scenario 1: As a local business owner, how can I find out about upcoming networking events to expand my professional contacts?

Scenario 2: As a resident, where can I learn more about serving on the chamber's board of directors and find contact information to inquire about potential opportunities?

Scenario 3: As an entrepreneur researching business opportunities in Jeonju, where can I get information on recent population trends and economic indicators in the area?
